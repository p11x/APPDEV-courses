Cards with Content

Cards can contain various types of content including images, text, lists, badges, avatars, and more. Understanding how to combine these elements effectively creates rich card components.

Standard Example: Content-Rich Cards

<!-- Card with List -->
<div class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-md p-6">
    <h3 class="font-bold mb-4">Recent Activity</h3>
    <ul class="space-y-3">
        <li class="flex items-center gap-3">
            <div class="w-2 h-2 bg-blue-500 rounded-full"></div>
            <span class="text-gray-600">New user registration</span>
        </li>
        <li class="flex items-center gap-3">
            <div class="w-2 h-2 bg-green-500 rounded-full"></div>
            <span class="text-gray-600">Project completed</span>
        </li>

</div>
