Card Structure

Cards are versatile containers used to organize content into grouped sections. A card typically contains a header, body, and optional footer. In Tailwind CSS, cards are built using bg-white, rounded corners, shadows, and padding utilities.

Card Components:

  • Container: bg-white rounded-lg shadow
  • Header: Title, metadata, actions
  • Body: Main content area
  • Footer: Action buttons, links
  • Media: Optional image/hero section

Standard Example: Basic Card Layouts

<!-- Simple Card -->
<div class="max-w-sm bg-white rounded-lg shadow-md p-6">
    <h3 class="text-xl font-bold text-gray-900 mb-2">Card Title</h3>
    <p class="text-gray-600">Card content goes here.</p>
</div>

<!-- Card with Header and Body -->
<div class="max-w-sm bg-white rounded-lg shadow-md">
    <div class="px-6 py-4 border-b">
        <h3 class="text-lg font-semibold text-gray-900">Header</h3>
    </div>
    <div class="px-6 py-4">
        <p class="text-gray-600">Body content.</p>
    </div>
</div>

<!-- Card with All Sections -->
<div class="max-w-sm bg-white rounded-lg shadow-md overflow-hidden">
    <img src="..." alt="Card image" class="w-full h-48 object-cover"/>
    <div class="px-6 py-4">
        <h3 class="text-xl font-bold mb-2">Title</h3>
        <p class="text-gray-600">Description.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="px-6 py-4 bg-gray-50 border-t">
        <button class="text-blue-600 hover:underline">Action</button>
    </div>
</div>
