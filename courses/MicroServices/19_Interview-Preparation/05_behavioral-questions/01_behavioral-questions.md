# Behavioral Questions for Microservices Interviews

## Overview

Behavioral questions assess your soft skills, teamwork, and past experiences. Prepare using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).

## Common Questions

### Q1: Tell me about a time you dealt with a production outage

**Focus**: Incident response, communication, prioritization

**Sample Answer**:
- Situation: Major service outage during peak hours
- Action: Led cross-team response, implemented fix
- Result: Restored service in 30 minutes, <5% impact

### Q2: Describe a time you had to convince the team to adopt a new pattern

**Focus**: Influence, technical leadership

### Q3: Tell me about a microservices migration you were part of

**Focus**: Migration experience, challenges faced
