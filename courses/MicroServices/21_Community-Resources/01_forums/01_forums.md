# Community Resources: Microservices Ecosystem

## Overview

The microservices community provides numerous resources for learning, networking, and staying updated with best practices. This guide catalogs the most valuable community resources, including forums, conferences, newsletters, and open-source projects.

### Forums and Discussion

1. **Microservices Slack Community**
   - 10,000+ members
   - Real-time discussions
   - Expert AMAs
   - Join: slack.microservices.com

2. **Reddit r/microservices**
   - News and articles
   - Questions and answers
   - Project showcases

3. **Stack Overflow**
   - Tag: microservices
   - 5000+ questions
   - Expert answers

### Newsletter

   - Best practices

2. **The Microservices Digest**
   - Monthly deep dives
   - Tutorial roundups
