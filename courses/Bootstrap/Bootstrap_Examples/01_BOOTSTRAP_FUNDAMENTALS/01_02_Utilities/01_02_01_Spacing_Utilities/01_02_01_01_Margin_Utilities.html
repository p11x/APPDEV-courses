Bootstrap 5 - Margin Utilities

Bootstrap provides margin utility classes to add or remove margins on elements. The format is: .m{side}-{size}

1. All-Side Margins (.m-{size})
.m-0 — No margin
.m-1 — margin: 0.25rem
.m-2 — margin: 0.5rem
.m-3 — margin: 1rem
.m-4 — margin: 1.5rem
.m-5 — margin: 3rem
.m-auto — Horizontally centered
2. Directional Margins
.mt-3 — margin-top: 1rem
.mb-3 — margin-bottom: 1rem
.ms-3 — margin-start (left in LTR): 1rem
.me-3 — margin-end (right in LTR): 1rem
.mx-3 — margin-left & margin-right: 1rem
.my-3 — margin-top & margin-bottom: 1rem
3. Centering with .mx-auto
.mx-auto .w-50 — Horizontally centered at 50% width
.mx-auto .w-25 — Horizontally centered at 25% width
