Responsive Classes

Format: .border-{sm|md|lg|xl|xxl}-{property}

All border, border-0, color, and width classes support responsive infixes.

ExampleEffect

.border-lg-0Remove all borders at lg+
.border-sm-primaryPrimary color at sm+
