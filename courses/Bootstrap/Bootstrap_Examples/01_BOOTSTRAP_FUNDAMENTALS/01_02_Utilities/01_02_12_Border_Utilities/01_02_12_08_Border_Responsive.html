Responsive Border Classes

Add borders at specific breakpoints: .border-{sm|md|lg|xl|xxl}-{property}

  • .border-sm-top - Add top border at sm breakpoint
  • .border-md-0 - Remove all borders at md breakpoint
  • .border-lg-primary - Apply primary color at lg breakpoint
