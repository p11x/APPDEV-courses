Font Style: Italic & Normal

Use .fst-italic and .fst-normal to control italic styling.

.fst-italic - This text is displayed in italic style.

.fst-normal - This text is displayed in normal (non-italic) style.

Text Transform Utilities

Control text capitalization without changing the source content.

.text-lowercase - THIS ENTIRE TEXT BECOMES LOWERCASE

.text-uppercase - this entire text becomes uppercase

.text-capitalize - each word starts with a capital letter

The Art of Typography

Published on January 15, 2026

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed.

"Good typography is invisible. Bad typography is everywhere." - Oliver Reichenstein

Key Principles

  • Hierarchy: Use bold and light weights
  • Contrast: Combine uppercase with normal case
  • Spacing: Maintain consistent line height

Old version: Typography is just about choosing fonts.

Updated: Typography encompasses much more than font selection.
