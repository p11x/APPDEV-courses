Bootstrap 5 Text Alignment Utilities

Basic Text Alignment

Use .text-start, .text-center, and .text-end.

.text-start - Left aligned text (default)

.text-center - Center aligned text

.text-end - Right aligned text

Responsive Text Alignment

Alignment changes at breakpoints. Resize your browser to see the effect.

.text-start .text-md-center .text-lg-end
Left on small screens, center on medium, right on large.

.text-center (all breakpoints)
Always centered regardless of screen size.

Class XS SM (576px+) MD (768px+) LG (992px+) XL (1200px+)
.text-start Left Left Left Left Left
.text-sm-center Left Center Center Center Center
.text-md-end Left Left Right Right Right
