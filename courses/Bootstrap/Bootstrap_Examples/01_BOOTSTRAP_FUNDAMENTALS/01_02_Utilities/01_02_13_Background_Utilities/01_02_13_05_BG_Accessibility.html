WCAG Compliance for Background Colors

WCAG Contrast Requirements

  • Normal text (AA): 4.5:1 contrast ratio
  • Large text (AA): 3:1 contrast ratio
  • UI components (AA): 3:1 contrast ratio
  • Enhanced (AAA): 7:1 for normal text

Color Blindness Considerations

~8% of men have some form of color blindness. Don't rely solely on background color to convey meaning. Always add icons, text labels, or patterns.
