Bootstrap 5 Background Color Utilities

Contextual Background Colors

Use .bg-{color} classes. Text color is auto-adjusted for readability.

.bg-primary - Primary background (#0d6efd)
.bg-secondary - Secondary background (#6c757d)
.bg-success - Success background (#198754)
.bg-danger - Danger background (#dc3545)
.bg-warning - Warning background (#ffc107)
.bg-info - Info background (#0dcaf0)
.bg-light - Light background (#f8f9fa)
.bg-dark - Dark background (#212529)
.bg-body - Body background color
.bg-transparent - Transparent background
.bg-white - White background
.bg-black - Black background

Contrast Background Colors (.text-bg-*)

.text-bg-{color} sets both background and appropriate text color.

.text-bg-primary - Auto white text on primary bg
.text-bg-secondary - Auto white text on secondary bg
.text-bg-success - Auto white text on success bg
.text-bg-danger - Auto white text on danger bg
.text-bg-warning - Auto dark text on warning bg
.text-bg-info - Auto dark text on info bg
.text-bg-light - Auto dark text on light bg
.text-bg-dark - Auto white text on dark bg

Background Emphasis Colors

Subtle tinted backgrounds using emphasis variants.

.bg-primary-subtle - Light primary tint
.bg-secondary-subtle - Light secondary tint
.bg-success-subtle - Light success tint
.bg-danger-subtle - Light danger tint
.bg-warning-subtle - Light warning tint
.bg-info-subtle - Light info tint
.bg-light-subtle - Light subtle tint
.bg-dark-subtle - Dark subtle tint
