Breakpoint-Specific Overflow Utilities

Responsive Overflow Classes

Bootstrap allows you to apply overflow utilities at specific breakpoints:

  • .overflow-sm-auto - auto overflow on sm (576px) and up
  • .overflow-md-auto - auto overflow on md (768px) and up
  • .overflow-lg-auto - auto overflow on lg (992px) and up
  • .overflow-xl-auto - auto overflow on xl (1200px) and up
  • .overflow-xxl-auto - auto overflow on xxl (1400px) and up
