📚 What are Bootstrap Badges?

Bootstrap badges are small labeling components used to:

  • Counts - Notification numbers
  • Labels - Category tags
  • Status - Status indicators
  • Highlights - Important markers

Badges are lightweight and can be placed anywhere - in headings, buttons, or next to text!
