🎨 Bootstrap Combined Colors

Learn how to combine text and background colors effectively for better visual design.

📚 Why Combine Text & Background Colors?

When combining text and background colors, you need to ensure proper contrast for readability. Bootstrap provides:

  • Text color classes (.text-*) for coloring text
  • Background color classes (.bg-*) for coloring backgrounds
  • Automatic contrast - dark backgrounds need light text

Golden Rule: Use light text (.text-white) on dark backgrounds, and dark text (.text-dark) on light backgrounds!

✅ Proper Text + Background Combinations

Here are recommended combinations that ensure good contrast:

Primary Background

Use .text-white for contrast on dark backgrounds.

Success Background

White text works great on green backgrounds.

Danger Background

White text ensures readability on red.

Dark Background

White text on black/dark backgrounds.

Warning Background

Use .text-dark for contrast on yellow.

Info Background

Dark text reads well on cyan.

Light Background

Dark text on white/light backgrounds.

White Background

Dark text on pure white backgrounds.
