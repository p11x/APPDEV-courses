    💡 Pro Tips

    • Use Semantic Colors: Use .text-success for positive messages, .text-danger for errors
    • Lead for Impact: Use .lead for introductory paragraphs on landing pages
    • Display for Heroes: Use .display-* for big hero section headings
    • Keep It Readable: Don't use all caps or excessive colors
    • Mobile First: Test your typography on small screens

    ✅ Best Practices

    1. Consistency: Use the same heading hierarchy throughout your page
    2. Contrast: Ensure text is readable against its background
    3. Hierarchy: Use font size/weight to show content importance
    4. White Space: Add proper margins and padding for readability
    5. Avoid Overuse: Don't use too many colors or font styles
