📚 What are Bootstrap Cards?

Cards are flexible, extensible content containers that provide a standardized way to display content. They can contain:

  • Headers - Optional top section
  • Body - Main content area
  • Footers - Optional bottom section
  • Images - Top or background images
  • Links - Clickable card links
