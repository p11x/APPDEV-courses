📚 Navbar Toggler (Hamburger Menu)

The toggler is the hamburger icon that appears on mobile devices:

  • .navbar-toggler - The button element
  • .navbar-toggler-icon - The hamburger icon
  • data-bs-toggle="collapse" - Bootstrap attribute
  • Only visible on screens smaller than the expand breakpoint

⚙️ How It Works

  1. On Large Screens (≥992px): Navigation links are visible, toggler is hidden
  2. On Small Screens (<992px): Navigation collapses, toggler appears
  3. Click Toggler: Opens/closes the collapsed menu
Key Attributes:
  • data-bs-toggle="collapse" - Enables toggle functionality
  • data-bs-target="#id" - Links button to collapsible content

📱 Different Breakpoints

Change when the navbar expands:

  • .navbar-expand-sm - Expand at 576px+
  • .navbar-expand-md - Expand at 768px+
  • .navbar-expand-lg - Expand at 992px+ (default)
  • .navbar-expand-xl - Expand at 1200px+
  • .navbar-expand - Always expanded (no collapse)
