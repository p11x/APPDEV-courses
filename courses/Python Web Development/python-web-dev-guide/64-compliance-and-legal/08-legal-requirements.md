# Legal Requirements

## What You'll Learn

- Data protection laws
- Industry-specific regulations
- Legal considerations for web apps

## Prerequisites

- Completed `07-audit-logging.md`

## Major Data Protection Laws

| Regulation | Region | Key Points |
|------------|--------|------------|
| GDPR | EU | User consent, data portability |
| CCPA | California | Right to know, delete |
| LGPD | Brazil | Similar to GDPR |
| PIPL | China | Data localization |

## Industry Regulations

- **Healthcare**: HIPAA (US)
- **Finance**: PCI DSS, SOX
- **Education**: FERPA
- **Children**: COPPA (US)

## Legal Checklist

- [ ] Privacy policy published
- [ ] Terms of service published
- [ ] Cookie consent mechanism
- [ ] Data processing agreements
- [ ] Security measures documented
- [ ] Breach notification procedures
- [ ] Data retention policies

## Summary

- Understand applicable regulations
- Implement required protections
- Document compliance efforts
