# MongoDB Atlas - Requirements

## Account Requirements

- MongoDB account (free to create)
- Email verification
- Credit card NOT required for free tier

## System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- For local tools: Node.js, Python, or other drivers

## Free Tier Limitations

- 512 MB storage limit
- Shared RAM (not dedicated)
- Single node (no replication)
- Limited to one free cluster per account
- 100 connections maximum
